Discover Uyo

Visiting a city for the first time can be difficult and confusing, Here are some of the most exciting places in uyo you should be eager to visit. They include historical sites, cultural buildings, beach, mall and even a learning site.

Days Since Last Visits:

Ibom Mall

This is a state-owned event centre where social, economic and political events are hosted. A magnificent structure with state-of-art facilities, the hall has played host to a number of important events. It is also a popular landmark in the town.It is located at IBB Avenue,uyo

Le Meridien Resort

Ibom Icon Hotel & Golf Resort has rooms decorated with lush essence and comfort. They are categorised into Executive Room, Honeymoon Package, Deluxe Room, Junior Suite, Executive Suite and Diplomatic. The rooms are equipped with air conditioners, and more

Ibeno Beach

Ibeno Beach stretches for about 30 kilometres from Ibeno to James Town along the Atlantic coastline of Akwa Ibom State in Nigeria. It is said to be the longest beach in West Africa. With its beautiful coastline, Ibeno provides endless natural facilities for tourism, water sporting, beach soccer and general boating.

Ibom Museum

This edifice is located in the city of Uyo, in the famous Udo Udoma Avenue. The museum has a notable collection of historic pictures and it was an inspiration of Arc Victor Attah the past Governor of Akwa Ibom state. It was commission by Arc (Obong) Victor Attah on Tuesday, 22nd May 2007

Ibom Plaza

Located in the heart of Uyo, Ibom plaza is a wonderful place for people to meet and connect. It has a clean open environment with seats made like staircase. A big screen is also available here for residents to watch sports or other events together. This was created to increase bond amongst residents

Ibom Tropicana

Surrounded by banks and a military barrack, this mall is at the right spot. It was recently renovated in 2020 to make more space in it. It has everything a mall has from hair saloon, escalators, cinemas to several shopping sections to make purchases

Ibom Stadium

The stadium which currently is the most sophisticated in Nigeria was a goodbye gift by the last governor to the state. It is also referred to as the the "Nest of Champions" and the seat capacity is 35,000. A lot of international matches occur here and can be fun for sport lovers

Ibom E-library

The Ibom Electronic library takes library experience to the next level, being the first in Africa as at the time it was constructed. The library has a capacity of over 1500 computer seats and each has software that has the latest information on all aspects of study

University of Uyo

Although being a federal university, it resides in the Nwaniba area of Uyo and can be a great place to visit. The department of Art does a great job to litre the school campus with wonderful sculptures they make from their projects and they look pleasing to the eyes
