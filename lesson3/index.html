Our Purpose

The purpose of Uyo chamber of commerce website is to make the navigations of visitors to uyo easy and without stress. Uyo is the capital city of Akwa Ibom state which was the fastest developing city in Nigeria for about 3 consecutive years. It's a city with so many wonderful sites like an electronic library, a golf course and hiking sites, fancy bridges and flyovers, a 35,000 capacity soccer stadium for internationa matches and so much more.

This website will allow visitors and residents to easily navigate their way to Uyo's top places like malls, dinners, markets, hotels, recreational sites and more. The user would be able to select categories and filter out the best using the ratings features and pricing. Amazing pictures of the location would also make it easy for recognition once they arrive. Lastly, a map feature that would allow distance calculation and easy navigation.

The domain name found available at the time of development is uyocommerce.com
